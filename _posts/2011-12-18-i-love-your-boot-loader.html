GRUB on a cigarette machine 

According to Ruben the GRUB (and probably POSIX) powered cigarette machine can be found in an Amsterdam bar. What an interesting embedded system! After seeing this it's impossible not to show sympathy for the Netherlands. Most other embedded systems that I have seen in other countries are using Windows. Good job bar owners. I'll make sure to visit your bar for a beer :)
